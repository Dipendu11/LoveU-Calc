How to estimate asphalt

Calculating the amount of asphalt needed requires converting the volume of your driveway or parking lot into weight (tons). Asphalt is typically sold by the ton but installed by the cubic foot.

The Formula

We use the standard industry density of 145 lbs per cubic foot. The basic formula is:

(Length × Width × Depth) × 145 lbs = Total Weight

Our calculator also adds a standard compaction factor (asphalt shrinks when rolled) and a waste margin to ensure you don't run short.
